A full A1-style practice paper, shaped like the Center za slovenščino entry-level exam: reading (true/false on signs, matching ads, an email), listening, a reply-to-a-letter, and speaking. The real paper is all in Slovene — the English here is a learner aid. Aim for ≥60% on each marked part.

01

Reading 1 — true / false

· Branje 1 · JA/NE/ 4
Read the department-store directory, then mark each statement JA (true) or NE (false).
VELEBLAGOVNICA — nadstropja 3. nadstropje: igrače in otroška oblačila 2. nadstropje: moška oblačila 1. nadstropje: ženska oblačila pritličje: kozmetika in čevlji
show English
DEPARTMENT STORE — floors. 3rd floor: toys and children's clothes. 2nd floor: men's clothes. 1st floor: women's clothes. Ground floor: cosmetics and shoes.
Ženska oblačila so v prvem nadstropju.
Women's clothes are on the first floor.
✓ JA — “1. nadstropje: ženska oblačila” — true
Igrače dobite v pritličju.
You get toys on the ground floor.
✓ NE — toys are on the 3rd floor — false
Čevlje dobite v pritličju.
You get shoes on the ground floor.
✓ JA — “pritličje: … čevlji” — true
Moška oblačila so v tretjem nadstropju.
Men's clothes are on the third floor.
✓ NE — men's clothes are on the 2nd floor — false

Radi bi se naučili kuhati.

✓ A — tečaj kuhanja — the cooking course (A)

✓ B — soba — room to let (B)

Writing — reply to a letter

· Pisanje/ 8
Your friend Peter wrote to you. Write a reply (about 30 words): answer his questions, and greet him at the start and the end.
Hej! Zelo sem vesel, da prideš v Ljubljano. Kdaj prideš? Kako boš potoval — z avtom ali z vlakom? Koliko časa boš ostal? Ali pride kdo s tabo? Prosim, hitro mi odgovori. Lep pozdrav, Peter
show English
Hi! I'm very glad you're coming to Ljubljana. When are you coming? How will you travel — by car or by train? How long will you stay? Is anyone coming with you? Please reply quickly. Best, Peter
Write your reply to Peter.
  • Greet Peter at the start.
  • Say when you'll come and how you'll travel.
  • Say how long you'll stay and whether someone comes with you.
  • Sign off at the end.
