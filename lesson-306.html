The world of work

· enota · svet dela
describe the job — and the people — that you want
To join two ideas — 'the company that interested me' — Slovene uses the invariable relative pronoun ki. Because ki never changes, the case is carried by a little resumptive clitic (ga, jo, mu…) later in the clause: človek, ki ga poznam (the man whom I know — accusative), šef, ki mu zaupam (the boss I trust — dative). When 'ki' is the subject, no clitic is needed: podjetje, ki me zanima.
ki + povratni zaimek · the resumptive clitic
primer · exampleangleško
človek, ki ga poznamthe man (whom) I know
ženska, ki jo poznamthe woman I know
šef, ki mu zaupamthe boss I trust (dative)
the changing ending is highlighted
Podjetje, ki me zanima.The company that interests me. (ki = subject)no clitic
Služba, ki jo iščem.The job (that) I'm looking for.jo = accusative, f
Sodelavci, ki jih imam.The colleagues (that) I have.jih = accusative, pl

text

· describe the job — and the people — that you want
Iskala sem novo službo več mesecev. Poslala sem veliko prijav. Končno me je poklicalo podjetje, ki me je zanimalo. Razgovor je vodila prijazna šefica, ki mi je postavila veliko vprašanj. Povedala sem o svojih izkušnjah in zakaj si želim to delo. Čez teden so me obvestili, da sem dobila službo. Sodelavci, ki jih imam zdaj, so res prijazni.
show English
I looked for a new job for several months. I sent many applications. Finally a company that interested me called me. The interview was led by a kind boss, who asked me many questions. I talked about my experience and why I want this job. A week later they informed me that I'd got the job. The colleagues I have now are really kind.

speak

· say it aloud, then check a model
1. Say what you do and where you work.
model answer
Po poklicu sem inženir. Delam pri velikem podjetju v Ljubljani.
By profession I'm an engineer. I work for a big company in Ljubljana.
2. At an interview, describe one of your strengths (use 'ki').
model answer
Sem oseba, ki rada dela v skupini in ki hitro reši probleme.
I'm a person who likes working in a team and who solves problems quickly.
3. Say what kind of job you're looking for and why.
model answer
Iščem službo, ki je zanimiva in ki ponuja dobro plačo.
I'm looking for a job that is interesting and that offers good pay.
