slovenščina · about

about & credits

A free, open course for learning Slovene — from the alphabet (A1) right up to mastery (C2). It works entirely in your browser, installs as an app, runs offline, and needs no sign-up. Your progress is saved only on your own device.

📚 255 lessons · 10 practice exams · A1 → C2 · word bank · spaced-repetition review · progress dashboard

How it's made

All lessons, sentences, grammar explanations and example texts are written originally for this course. The teaching structure is informed by widely-used approaches to Slovene (such as Čas za slovenščino and Slovenščina na dlani) — their methods, never their content. The course is independent and not affiliated with any official institution. Pronunciations are simplified English-style respellings, not IPA.

Folk proverbs and idioms used are traditional / public domain. No copyrighted textbook, worksheet or literary work is reproduced.

A note on accuracy

This is a labour of love built with AI assistance, and a native-speaker review is ongoing. If you spot a mistake or something that reads oddly, please tell us — it genuinely helps.
