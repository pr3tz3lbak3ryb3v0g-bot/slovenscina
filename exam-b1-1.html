01

Reading

· Branje
Read Tomaž's message to his friend, then answer the questions.
Živjo, Marko! Končno imam novice. Dobil sem novo službo v Ljubljani in se moram preseliti. Stanovanje, ki sem ga našel, je blizu centra, a je malo drago. Delo se začne prvega oktobra. Čeprav me je malo strah, sem zelo vesel. Upam, da me prideš obiskat. Lep pozdrav, Tomaž
show English
Hi, Marko! I finally have news. I got a new job in Ljubljana and I have to move. The flat I found is near the centre, but a bit expensive. The work starts on the 1st of October. Although I'm a little scared, I'm very happy. I hope you'll come to visit. Best wishes, Tomaž

What did Tomaž get?

04

Writing — a complaint letter

· Pisanje/ 20
You bought a phone online but it arrived damaged. Write a formal complaint to the shop (60–80 words).
  • Begin formally (Spoštovani …).
  • Say what you bought and what is wrong with it.
  • Say what you want — a refund or a replacement.
  • Ask for a reply and close formally with your name.
show a model answer
Spoštovani, pred tednom dni sem pri vas kupil telefon prek spleta. Žal je izdelek prispel poškodovan — zaslon je počen. Prosim za zamenjavo ali vračilo denarja. Prilagam račun in fotografijo. Prosim, da mi čim prej odgovorite. Lep pozdrav, Ana NovakDear Sir/Madam, a week ago I bought a phone from you online. Unfortunately the product arrived damaged — the screen is cracked. I request a replacement or a refund. I enclose the receipt and a photo. Please reply as soon as possible. Kind regards, Ana Novak

05

Speaking

· Govorjenje
1. Tell us about a problem you had recently and how you solved it (use the past tense).
show a model answer
Prejšnji teden se mi je pokvaril avto. Najprej sem poklical mehanika, a je bil predrag. Potem sem našel drugega, ki ga je popravil hitro in poceni. Na koncu je bilo vse v redu.Last week my car broke down. First I called a mechanic, but he was too expensive. Then I found another, who fixed it quickly and cheaply. In the end everything was fine.
2. Give your opinion: Ali je življenje v mestu boljše kot na vasi? (Is city life better than village life?)
show a model answer
Po mojem mnenju ima vsako svoje prednosti. V mestu je več dela in zabave, na vasi pa je mirno in zdravo. Sam imam raje vas, ker imam rad naravo.In my opinion each has its advantages. In the city there's more work and entertainment, while the village is quiet and healthy. I myself prefer the village because I like nature.
3. Role-play: you're at the bank opening an account. Ask about the fees, a card and online banking.
show a model answer
Dober dan, rad bi odprl račun. Kakšni so mesečni stroški? Ali dobim kartico? In ali ponujate spletno bančništvo?Good day, I'd like to open an account. What are the monthly costs? Do I get a card? And do you offer online banking?
