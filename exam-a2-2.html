01

Reading

· Branje
Read the station notice, then answer the questions.
OBVESTILO Vlak za Maribor odpelje s prvega tira ob 14.30. Vožnja traja dve uri. Vozovnico lahko kupite na blagajni ali na avtomatu. Otroci do šestega leta potujejo brezplačno.
show English
NOTICE. The train to Maribor leaves from platform 1 at 14:30. The journey takes two hours. You can buy a ticket at the ticket office or from the machine. Children up to the age of six travel free.
Kdaj odpelje vlak?
When does the train leave?
✓ ob 14.30 — “… odpelje … ob 14.30.”
Kako dolgo traja vožnja?
How long is the journey?
✓ dve uri — “Vožnja traja dve uri.” (the dual)
Kje lahko kupiš vozovnico?
Where can you buy a ticket?
✓ na blagajni ali avtomatu — “… na blagajni ali na avtomatu.”
Kdo potuje brezplačno?
Who travels free?
✓ otroci do šestega leta — “Otroci do šestega leta …”

Bonus — grammar practice

· Raba jezika
Choose the correct form for each gap.
ℹ️ Not a part of the real exam — the official test checks grammar inside the reading, writing and speaking. Extra targeted practice.
Januar je ___ mesec.
January is the first month. (ordinal)
✓ prvi — ordinal: prvi = first
To je ___ hiša.
This is a big house. (agreement, f)
✓ velika — feminine → velika
Jaz ___ doma.
I will be home. (future)
✓ bom — future: bom
Mi ___ slovensko.
We speak Slovenian. (plural present)
✓ govorimo — mi → govorimo
Vsak dan se ___ slovensko.
Every day I learn Slovenian. (reflexive verb)
✓ učim — učiti se → (jaz) se učim
___ iti domov.
I must go home. (modal)
✓ Moram — morati: moram + infinitive
04

Writing — an apology

· Pisanje/ 10
You go to a Slovenian course but can't attend for two weeks. Write an apology (40–50 words) to your teacher. Begin and end the letter appropriately.
Write your apology to the teacher.
  • Begin appropriately and apologise.
  • Say which course you attend and which days.
  • Say when and why you'll be away.
  • Say when you'll return and ask for the course materials (gradivo).
show a model answer
Spoštovana učiteljica, lepo se opravičujem. Obiskujem tečaj slovenščine ob ponedeljkih in sredah. Naslednja dva tedna me ne bo, ker grem na službeno pot v tujino. Vrnem se 20. junija. Prosim, pošljite mi učno gradivo. Hvala in lep pozdrav, Ana KovačDear teacher, I sincerely apologise. I attend the Slovenian course on Mondays and Wednesdays. I'll be away for the next two weeks because I'm going on a business trip abroad. I'll return on 20 June. Please send me the course materials. Thank you and best regards, Ana Kovač

05

Speaking

· Govorjenje
1. Talk about your work or studies: what you do and what a typical day is like.
show a model answer
Delam kot učitelj v šoli. Vsak dan vstanem ob šestih, grem v službo z avtobusom in delam do treh. Popoldne pripravljam ure.I work as a teacher in a school. Every day I get up at six, go to work by bus and work until three. In the afternoon I prepare lessons.
2. Role-play: plan a trip with a friend. Suggest where to go, how to travel, and when.
show a model answer
Pojdiva v soboto na morje! Lahko greva z vlakom, odpelje ob osmih. Vzemiva kopalke in malico. Kaj misliš?Let's go to the seaside on Saturday! We can go by train, it leaves at eight. Let's take swimsuits and a snack. What do you think?
3. Describe your daily routine from morning to evening.
show a model answer
Zjutraj vstanem in se umijem. Za zajtrk pijem kavo. Dopoldne delam, opoldne imam kosilo. Zvečer gledam televizijo in ob enajstih grem spat.In the morning I get up and wash. For breakfast I drink coffee. In the morning I work, at noon I have lunch. In the evening I watch TV and at eleven I go to sleep.
