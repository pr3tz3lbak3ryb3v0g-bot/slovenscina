01

Reading

· Branje
Read the shop's opening hours, then answer the questions.
TRGOVINA SADJE IN ZELENJAVA Odprto: od ponedeljka do petka: 8.00–19.00 sobota: 8.00–13.00 nedelja: zaprto
show English
SHOP — FRUIT AND VEGETABLES. Open: Monday to Friday 8am–7pm; Saturday 8am–1pm; Sunday: closed.
Kdaj je trgovina zaprta?
When is the shop closed?
✓ v nedeljo — “nedelja: zaprto”
Do kdaj je odprta v soboto?
Until when is it open on Saturday?
✓ do enih — “sobota: 8.00–13.00” (1pm)
Ali je odprta v petek?
Is it open on Friday?
✓ da — “od ponedeljka do petka”

Writing — fill the form

· Pisanje/ 5
You want to sign up for a computer course. Fill in the registration form (you can invent the details) and write two questions for the organiser — ask about the location and the price.
PRIJAVNICA — TEČAJ RAČUNALNIŠTVA Ime in priimek: __________ Starost: __________ Poklic: __________ Telefon / e-pošta: __________ Vaši vprašanji: __________
show English
REGISTRATION — COMPUTER COURSE. Full name: ___ Age: ___ Occupation: ___ Phone/email: ___ Your two questions: ___
Fill in the form and write your two questions.
  • Fill in name, age, occupation and contact.
  • Write a question about the location (kje).
  • Write a question about the price (koliko).
show a model answer
Ime in priimek: Ana Kovač Starost: 28 Poklic: učiteljica Telefon: 040 123 456 Vaši vprašanji: Kje je tečaj? Koliko stane tečaj?Name: Ana Kovač. Age: 28. Occupation: teacher. Phone: 040 123 456. Your questions: Where is the course? How much does the course cost?

05

Speaking

· Govorjenje
1. Talk about your family: who is in it and what they do.
show a model answer
Imam mamo, očeta in sestro. Mama je učiteljica, oče dela v trgovini. Moja sestra hodi v šolo.I have a mum, a dad and a sister. Mum is a teacher, dad works in a shop. My sister goes to school.
2. Answer: Kje živiš in kakšno je tvoje mesto? (Where do you live and what's your town like?)
show a model answer
Živim v Celju. Mesto je majhno, a lepo. Imamo park, trgovine in lepo reko.I live in Celje. The town is small but nice. We have a park, shops and a beautiful river.
3. Role-play: ask a stranger how to get to the train station.
show a model answer
Oprostite, kako pridem do železniške postaje? Je daleč?Excuse me, how do I get to the train station? Is it far?
