01

Reading

· Branje
Read this short article about Slovenian habits, then answer the questions.
V Sloveniji je kava več kot le pijača — je priložnost za druženje. Ljudje se pogosto dobijo 'na kavi', tudi če ne pijejo kave. V mestih je veliko kavarn, kjer se prijatelji pogovarjajo cele ure. Konec tedna je čas za izlete v naravo, saj imajo Slovenci radi gore in hribe.
show English
In Slovenia coffee is more than just a drink — it's a chance to socialise. People often meet 'for a coffee', even if they don't drink coffee. In the cities there are many cafés where friends talk for hours. The weekend is a time for trips into nature, as Slovenes love the mountains and hills.
Kaj pomeni kava v Sloveniji?
What does coffee mean in Slovenia?
✓ priložnost za druženje — “… je priložnost za druženje.”
Kje se prijatelji pogovarjajo?
Where do friends talk?
✓ v kavarnah — “… v kavarnah … cele ure.”
Kaj je čas za konec tedna?
What is the weekend a time for?
✓ izlete v naravo — “… čas za izlete v naravo …”
Kaj imajo Slovenci radi?
What do Slovenes love?
✓ gore in hribe — “… imajo radi gore in hribe.”

04

Writing — a job application

· Pisanje/ 20
You saw a job ad for a waiter in a restaurant. Write a short application (60–80 words).
  • Begin formally (Spoštovani …).
  • Say which job you are applying for.
  • Describe your experience and why you are suitable.
  • Ask for an interview and close formally with your name.
show a model answer
Spoštovani, v vašem oglasu sem opazil, da iščete natakarja. Rad bi se prijavil na to delovno mesto. Imam dve leti izkušenj v gostinstvu in rad delam z ljudmi. Sem zanesljiv, prijazen in se hitro učim. Vesel bi bil priložnosti za razgovor. Lep pozdrav, Marko KraljDear Sir/Madam, in your ad I noticed that you're looking for a waiter. I'd like to apply for this position. I have two years of experience in catering and I like working with people. I'm reliable, friendly and learn quickly. I'd be glad of the chance for an interview. Kind regards, Marko Kralj

05

Speaking

· Govorjenje
1. Describe your typical week (use the present tense and time expressions).
show a model answer
Med tednom vstanem ob šestih in grem v službo. Popoldne se ukvarjam s športom ali berem. Zvečer kuham večerjo in gledam film. Konec tedna pa rad potujem ali obiščem družino.During the week I get up at six and go to work. In the afternoon I do sport or read. In the evening I cook dinner and watch a film. At the weekend I like to travel or visit family.
2. Give your opinion: Ali je pomembno učiti se tuje jezike? Zakaj? (Is it important to learn foreign languages? Why?)
show a model answer
Po mojem mnenju je zelo pomembno. Tuji jeziki nam pomagajo pri delu in potovanju. Poleg tega lahko spoznamo nove ljudi in kulture. Zato se učim slovensko.In my opinion it's very important. Foreign languages help us with work and travel. Besides, we can meet new people and cultures. That's why I'm learning Slovenian.
3. Role-play: you're at the doctor's. Describe your symptoms and ask what to do.
show a model answer
Dober dan. Že tri dni me boli glava in imam vročino. Slabo spim in nimam apetita. Kaj mi svetujete? Ali potrebujem zdravila?Good day. For three days I've had a headache and a fever. I sleep badly and have no appetite. What do you advise? Do I need medicine?
