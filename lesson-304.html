My free time

· enota · moj prosti čas
say where you're going, what you like, what you did
Where to? = Kam? — the destination goes in the tožilnik (accusative) after v or na: grem v kino, grem na koncert. To say you like doing something, use rad (m) / rada (f): rad berem. For the past, take biti + the -l participle: gledal sem (I watched). How often? pogosto (often), včasih (sometimes), nikoli (never).
Kaj je to? → Kam greš? (imenovalnik → tožilnik)

V prostem času rad počnem veliko stvari. Pogosto poslušam glasbo in berem knjige. Ob koncu tedna grem v kino ali na koncert. Včeraj sem bil na nogometni tekmi. Bilo je super! Nikoli mi ni dolgčas, ker imam veliko hobijev.
show English
In my free time I like doing many things. I often listen to music and read books. At the weekend I go to the cinema or a concert. Yesterday I was at a football match. It was great! I'm never bored, because I have many hobbies.
